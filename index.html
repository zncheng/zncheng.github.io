Biography

Zhinan Cheng is a Phd student under the supervision of Prof. Patrick P. C. Lee in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong. He is also a member of the Applied Distributed Systems Lab (ADSLab). He received his M.S degree in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2017 where he worked with Prof. Xi Li at the Embedded System Lab (ESLab). And he received his B.S degree in Computer Science from South China Normal University (SCNU) in 2014.

His current research interest is to build dependable stream processing systems (with emphasis on performance and fault tolerance) to support real-time analytics (e.g., online machine learning, data synopsis). He used to focus on power and performance optimization for mobile platform when he was with USTC.
